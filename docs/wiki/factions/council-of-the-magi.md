## Description

The **Council of the Magi** is an imperial body that claims authority over matters arcane within the oni Imperium of Chu Ye—specifically, **arcane ruins, geomantic sites, and magical artifacts of imperial concern**. References to the Council have been scattered and sparse, but consistent in one thing: the Council's jurisdiction is cited whenever someone in office wants to override a local authority in an arcane matter.

[Magistrate Kurosawa](../npcs/magistrate-kurosawa.md) is the only confirmed member the party has encountered. He claims to belong to, or represent, the Council in [Willowshore](../locations/willowshore.md), and has repeatedly invoked its authority to direct actions that [Mayor Masru's](../npcs/mayor-masru.md) municipal office could not reach.

---

## Claimed Authority

From Kurosawa's statements (public and in open court):

- **Arcane ruins**, such as the [Gosembiki ruins](../locations/gosembiki-ruins.md), fall under Council jurisdiction rather than municipal authority
- **Artifacts of imperial arcane concern** (including whatever the Yeshou family was accused of stealing)
- **Geomantic sites and ley line infrastructure**
- **The right to commission "measuring devices"** or equivalent research instruments without municipal review
- **The right to deem an expert "not an expert"** for courtroom purposes—e.g., disqualifying the device's own builder from speaking to its purpose

During the Rite of Open Reckoning in Session 8, when the party demanded an **impartial arcane expert**, Kurosawa simply said *"I am the Council of the Magi"*—asserting himself as the only expert whose testimony counted.

---

## Scope & Unknowns

The Council is cited often and seen never. Everything known is secondhand:

- **Structure** — Size, composition, hierarchy all unknown
- **Oversight** — Whether any authority (the Shogun? the Magi themselves?) regulates its agents is unclear
- **Other agents** — [Kurosawa's](../npcs/magistrate-kurosawa.md) plural *"devices"* (Yong under cross) suggests he is not the only Council operative installing equipment in Chu Ye
- **Relationship to the old gods** — Kurosawa's ritual at the [Mourndusk Willow](../locations/dark-woods.md) and the Mother of a Thousand Wings summoning are not obviously Council business. They may be his private project, or they may be the Council's actual agenda dressed as something else.
