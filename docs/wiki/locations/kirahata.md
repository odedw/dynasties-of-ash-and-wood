## Description

Kirahata (also rendered as Purahata) is a large coastal city on the southern shore of Chu Ye, several days' journey from [Willowshore](willowshore.md). It is known as a hedonistic pleasure city—a center of entertainment, gambling, and nightlife that attracts the wealthy and famous from across the region.

[Mayor Masru](../npcs/mayor-masru.md) hails from Kirahata, where he ran several businesses, attempted a career as a sumo competitor (earning the nickname "Sumo of Smiles"), and frequented establishments like **the Red Orchid**—a gentleman's club. He mentioned that [Meilin](../npcs/meilin.md) could "make herself and myself very wealthy" working there.
