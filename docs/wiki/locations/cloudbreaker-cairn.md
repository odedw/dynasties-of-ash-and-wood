## What is Known

Cloudbreaker Cairn is named in [Kurosawa's](../npcs/magistrate-kurosawa.md) recovered planning note as the **Stone** key — one of the five ley-line "veins" he intends to force into accord beneath the [Mother of a Thousand Wings](../npcs/magistrate-kurosawa.md#goals) ritual. The full passage:

> *"Cloudbreaker Cairn must be made to bow. Even stone yields when proper law is spoken with sufficient force."*

The phrase *"made to bow"* and the elemental association with **Stone** suggest a megalithic site — a cairn, standing stones, or carved monolith — that has historically resisted oni or imperial authority and now must be subjugated, not destroyed. The reference to *"proper law... spoken with sufficient force"* implies the site is responsive to spoken or invoked authority of a particular kind, not raw violence.

## Open Questions

- **Where is it?** No location is given in the note; the party has not yet found a map or route to it
- **Who built it?** Cairns in Tian Xia mark older powers — pre-imperial spirits, mountain shrines, or burial grounds
- **What does it currently "speak"?** "Made to bow" implies it presently stands against something
- **In what order does Kurosawa intend to take it?** The note lists the keys but does not commit to a strict sequence after the Hollow
