## Description

Kuji is one of [Mido's](mido.md) children — present in the background of the family's appearances throughout the campaign, but never previously named. He had appeared as a quiet figure beside his mother in the [Yeshou estate](../locations/willowshore.md), the festival, and Silver Mist Lodge.

He came forward into the light in Session Nine when he climbed up through the floor of a Willowshore warehouse — through one of the family's old smuggling tunnels — to extract the imprisoned party.

---

## Personality

What the party has seen of Kuji so far is calm, competent, and unsurprised. He moves through the family's tunnel network with the easy confidence of someone who has done it many times — apologizing once for the narrow squeeze near the entrance, but otherwise treating the operation like a routine extraction.

He shares his mother's pragmatism. He does not posture or boast. When greeted with surprise that the family had a tunnel under the warehouse, he answered simply: *"It's come in very handy in the past."*

---

## Skills & Notes

- **Knows the tunnel network well** — led the party through a route that opened from the warehouse floor, descended into a natural passage, ran past the chasm crevice, and surfaced in a hidden cave outcropping west of town
- **Trusted operative** — sent ahead alone by [Mido](mido.md) to retrieve the party, with prearranged extraction at the cave mouth
- **Logistics & quartermastering** — at the end of the meeting, dispatched to retrieve gear from the family's stashes (full plate, healer's kits, sling, healing potions) and returned with most of what the party requested

---

## Relationships

### [Mido](mido.md)
His mother. He defers to her, but acts on his own judgment in the field — he was the one who decided exactly when and how to extract the party, on his own initiative.

### The Yeshou Family
Born into it. The family's tunnel network and arms caches appear to be his particular domain. Da Baishan's quip — *"I see it runs in the family"* — was met by Kuji with the small, knowing smile of someone who has heard it before.

### The Party
Sees them as allies of the family in a difficult moment. Treated them efficiently, without flattery, and was willing to scrounge specific gear for them on short notice — including a heavy plate sized for Boone.
