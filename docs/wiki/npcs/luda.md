## Description

Luda is an older woman who runs a modest shop in [Willowshore](../locations/willowshore.md). Her official trade is seamstress work—clothing repair, fabric, patches, general goods—but she also deals in rare plants, mushrooms, and alchemical ingredients. She has enough knowledge of alchemy and arcane components to supply spell-casters with the specialized inks, powders, and botanical materials needed for scrolls, spell transcription, and other magical crafting.

She is not a wizard or a practitioner, but she knows what the practitioners need and where to find it.

---

## Personality

Luda is blunt, suspicious of authority, and unapologetically hostile toward the oni presence in Willowshore. She does not mince words or soften her opinions. She views the entire oni regime as corrupt and self-serving.

---

## Relationships

### Donkey
Luda is one of Donkey's regular suppliers for spell components—special inks, crushed fungi, and other arcane crafting materials. They have a transactional but trusting relationship. When Donkey asked her to report on [Kurosawa's](magistrate-kurosawa.md) future purchases, she agreed without hesitation.

### [Magistrate Kurosawa](magistrate-kurosawa.md)
Kurosawa demanded arcane ingredients from Luda in high volume with extreme specificity. He paid her, but the interaction was cold and intimidating. She is deeply concerned about the purpose of the ingredients and considers the oni's presence in Willowshore a facade for something sinister.
