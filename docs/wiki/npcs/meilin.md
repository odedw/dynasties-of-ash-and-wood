## Description

Meilin is Willowshore's gravekeeper—normally seen in rough work clothes with matted hair, tending the cemetery grounds and maintaining the cricket cages that ward off spirits. She keeps a low profile and few in town pay her much attention.

That changed at the Festival of Returning Spirits, where she performed as one of five ceremonial dancers. After receiving a glow-up from [Radiant Willow](radiant-willow.md), Meilin stunned the entire town. Townspeople whispered that she might be the most beautiful young woman in Willowshore—a revelation that caught everyone by surprise except Willow, who had seen the potential all along.

## Relationships

### Littlefinger
Littlefinger helped Meilin at the graveyard during Session Two, assisting with the cricket cages after the undead encounter. [Radiant Willow](radiant-willow.md) noticed their interaction and considers them a potential match. Sadly, Littlefinger was infiltrating the Yeshou estate during Meilin's dance performance and missed her transformation entirely.

### [Radiant Willow](radiant-willow.md)
Willow gave Meilin her makeover for the festival and is clearly proud of the result. She considers the gravekeeper one of her finest works.
