# Cliché

*"What could be more cliché than a blind man giving wise advice in the mountains?"*

---

## Description

A small, scrawny figure hunched beside a stone-ringed coal fire on a narrow mountain landing. Tattered Tibet-style fur wraps for warmth. An old straw hat. **Cloth wraps wound entirely over the eyes.** No one in the party has ever seen what is, or is not, behind them.

He poked the coals with a walking stick when the party crested the switchback. Round, dark, almost-perfect spheres of stone — he kept them in the fire and would, on some private signal, reach down for one and use it as a gift, or a punctuation mark.

When the party left, his stones were still in the fire pit. The fire was still warm. The hermit was gone, and there were no tracks.

---

## Personality

Cliché speaks in compressed, oblique riddle-talk — only ever as much as the listener can carry, never the literal answer. He **never names anything by its proper name** within earshot. The Order is *"the eye."* Kurosawa is *"the smiling one"* and Cheng Yesho is referenced obliquely. The PCs are not given their own names back; instead they are labeled by what he sees in them:

| PC | Cliché's name for them |
|---|---|
| [Boone](../pcs/boone.md) | **Truth Hound** — *"You've come in search of the eye."* |
| [Da Baishan](../pcs/da-baishan.md) | *"One small scan."* |
| [Donkey](../pcs/donkey.md) | **The One Who Makes Sparks** |
| [Ginkgo](../pcs/ginkgo.md) | (greeted with a smile; offered the stone) |
| [Littlefinger](../pcs/littlefinger.md) | (greeted; not labeled aloud) |

He is unhurried, friendly in a clipped way, and faintly amused — a man who has waited for travelers like this before, and will wait again.

## What He Knows He Should Not

A society check (Donkey, 26) found that the hermit knows things a mountain recluse should not:

- He named the **smiling one** — meaning [Cheng Yesho](../npcs/the-smiling-one.md) — without ever being given the name
- He clearly knew about [Magistrate Kurosawa](../npcs/magistrate-kurosawa.md)
- He knew Da Baishan was on the road to **the eye** — without saying *Order* or *Palatine* aloud
- Asked about [Vujravati](../npcs/vujravati.md), he answered without hesitation: *"the celestial one — through cycles of time, has seen the rise and fall of civilizations and the gods themselves. A great asset is the celestial one."*

He does not appear in any Willowshore story Donkey or Da Baishan can recall, nor in any traveler's description of the southern mountains. Society 26 produced **no recognition at all.**

---

## The Three Roads

Asked which path the party should take, Cliché gave them, in order:

> *"Go south, find the eye. Go west, cut the root. Go back, save the town. All roads are dire. The question is not which road is safe. There is no safe road. The question is — which wound can you bear?"*

This is the first explicit framing of the campaign's three open fronts as **a single triage problem** rather than a sequence.

## Notable Quotes

> *"Late, you are early. Always rushing, truth seekers. Never asking if the truth wishes to be found, though. Come, come — sit."*

> *"You walk thinking truth awaits, like a lantern. But truth is not a lantern. Truth is a knife. It cuts rope, yes — and also the hand of those who uncovered too much."*

> *"A thing buried is not always buried by cowards. Sometimes buried by the wise."*

> *"Seek the cliff that has no door. Knock with the hand that doubts. And when truth speaks kindly, distrust it most."*

> *"Knowledge found. Keep nose to ground, mind to thread. That's good. Good — but careful. Some tracks follow you back."*

---

## Gifts

- A **warm hand-stone** to each of the four PCs who would take one — small, dark, perfectly round stones from the coals of his fire. They hold heat for hours; ideal for a pocket or a sleeve in mountain cold. No visible markings; no obvious magic. *"It'll warm your hands."*

He gave one to Da Baishan as he stood and tossed the rest. Then the party looked back, and he was gone.

## Open Questions

- Who or what is Cliché actually? A halfling oracle? Something pretending to be a halfling? An agent of the [Order](../factions/order-of-the-palatine-eye.md)? An agent of something the Order was founded to watch?
- Do the **stones** do more than warm hands? They were sitting in his fire as if waiting for someone.
- He knew Cheng Yesho's epithet without being told. He knew Vujravati. **What else does he know that he didn't volunteer?**
- *"Some tracks follow you back."* What did the party draw with them on the road south?
