## Description

Shen Takashi is a **legendary oni swordsman** who broke from the oni leadership an untold number of eons ago and wandered Chu Ye as a self-appointed arbiter of justice. The stories vary in the details, but the core is consistent: he fought *for the little folk and the workers* against the propaganda and cruelty of the dynasty.

He is called the **Silent Duelist** because—by most accounts—he did his work without speeches, without ceremony, and usually without a witness who lived to speak of it in detail. His reputation was carried by the dead as much as by the saved.

He has **not been seen in roughly twenty years.** Whether he died, withdrew, was imprisoned, or simply stopped being noticed is unknown.

---

## The Sword

Shen Takashi wielded a longsword the stories call **[Judgement's Edge](../locations/gosembiki-ruins.md#items-recovered)**. The blade is described as unnaturally sharp, marked with a **Rune of Striking**, and preserved in whatever condition its wielder last left it in. It was recovered by the party from the rubble of the [Gosembiki ruins](../locations/gosembiki-ruins.md) after the Session 8 collapse—entirely unblemished despite the devastation around it.

The sword is currently carried by [Da Baishan](../pcs/da-baishan.md), though confiscated at the end of Session 8 during the party's arrest.

## Open Questions

- **Why was his sword in the Gosembiki ruins?** The ruin is an arcane site—older than Shen Takashi by orders of magnitude—yet his weapon was buried there in a location a falling ruin could reveal. Did he go there to fight someone? To hide the sword? Is he still there, somewhere, unburied?
- **Is he dead?** Twenty years of absence is not proof. But nothing has been heard.
- **Did he know [Gujravati](gujravati.md)?** A good oni who fought against his own for the sake of the common people. A dragon who calls itself a witness against the rot. The parallel is striking. Connection unconfirmed.
- **Does Da Baishan now carry his legacy?** Intentionally or otherwise, the sword has chosen a new wielder.
