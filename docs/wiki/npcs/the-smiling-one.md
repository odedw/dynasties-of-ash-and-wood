## Description

Shang Yesho—known around Willowshore as "The Smiling One"—is [Migo's](migo.md) brother and a member of the Yeshou family. He is perpetually smiling, no matter the circumstances. When misfortune befalls the oni or their allies, his expression brightens. When the new regime stumbles, he seems quietly pleased. He is always in the background, always watching, always grinning.

He is a decorated military veteran who received a **gold medal with gem inlays** for bravery beyond the call of duty during his younger years. The medal was signed by a general and describes a distinguished act of valor.

---

## Knowledge

Shang has experience with the **Chthonic language** (the Devil's tongue) and was able to translate the term **Gosembiki** (五芒星, the Five Pointed Star) for his sister—the name [Magistrate Kurosawa](magistrate-kurosawa.md) gave to haunted ruins east of Willowshore.

He also claims to know [the true ending of the oldest story about the forest shadows](../locations/willowshore.md#the-forest-story)—a tale whose ending is never spoken aloud because it embarrasses the ruling family.

---

## Relationships

### [Migo](migo.md)
His sister and the family matriarch. Shang serves as a trusted advisor and translator, particularly for matters involving the Chthonic language.

### The Party
When Littlefinger returned his military medal, Shang came forward—still smiling—and took it back with obvious delight. He has not spoken directly to the party at length.
