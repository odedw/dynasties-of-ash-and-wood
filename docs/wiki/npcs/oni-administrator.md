## Description

A **small blue-skinned oni woman**, perhaps 5'6" — nothing like [Kurosawa's](magistrate-kurosawa.md) seven-foot menace or the [Mayor's](mayor-masru.md) girth. She carries **no visible weapons**: no nodachi, no katana like the red-skinned guardsmen wear. The party's read from the tree line was *"maybe a wizard, maybe an accountant"* — bookkeeping, paperwork, quiet authority.

What she lacks in stature she holds in **rank**. She moves with at least one guard escort, works out of the estate, and **issues orders that are obeyed** — even [Captain Akoto](captain-akoto.md) follows them. She is the third blue-skinned oni seen in Willowshore, and like the other two, the red-skinned soldiery answers to her.

---

## Context of Her Arrival

The party spotted her while surveilling Willowshore from the northwest tree line (Session Twelve), after roughly a month away:

- The garrison has **doubled** — approximately **eight** red-skinned oni guards, up from four (one of whom died in the demon attack)
- **Kurosawa was nowhere to be seen** across hours of observation — a conspicuous absence, given that she now appears to run the estate he occupied
- The town otherwise looked eerily normal, going about its recovering business

Whether she is Kurosawa's subordinate, his replacement, his watcher, or something else is unknown.
