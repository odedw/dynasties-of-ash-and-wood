## Description

Kimmy is a young human girl, roughly ten to twelve years old—the same girl Boone rescued from the burning warehouse in Session One. She is brave enough to sprint across town to find help, and honest enough that her panic reads as genuine rather than performative.

Her family runs the **Cloud Paper Lodge**, a paper lantern shop whose fireworks were stored in the destroyed warehouse.

---

## Personality

Kimmy is earnest and resourceful for her age. When [Hong](hong.md) fell into the well, she didn't panic uselessly—she ran to find the one person she trusted to help: Boone, the dwarf who had already saved her once. She is loyal to Hong and clearly cares about him, though neither child seems ready to fully acknowledge what that means.

---

## Relationships

### Boone
Kimmy trusts Boone implicitly after he rescued her from the warehouse fire. She sought him out by name when Hong needed help.

### [Hong](hong.md)
Hong and Kimmy are close friends with hints of a childhood crush. Hong broke her festival lantern—her "first wish" lantern for the river ceremony—while trying to retrieve it from the well for her. When Da Baishan suggested Hong needed "a kiss to feel better," Kimmy gave him a shy peck on the cheek.
