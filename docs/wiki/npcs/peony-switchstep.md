## Description

Peony Switchstep is a halfling seamstress in [Willowshore](../locations/willowshore.md), known for her cheerful disposition and love of community gossip. [Radiant Willow](radiant-willow.md) considers her an ideal match for Da Baishan—reasoning that his stern nature would be complemented by someone who sees the best in everyone.
