## Description

Liwen is a middle-aged woman in her forties, with long hair streaked with gray and permanently dirt-stained fingertips from years of gardening. She runs a tiny shop in Willowshore—barely more than a cart—selling flowers, arrangements, and whatever she can grow or gather. She supplies [Radiant Willow](radiant-willow.md) with flowers for Dew Drop Petals.

---

## Personality

Liwen is a private, cautious person who keeps her opinions close. She does not gossip freely and tends to avoid discussing the oni or the political changes in town. This is not deception but self-preservation—she has lived in Willowshore for years, likes the way things are, and fears what the new leadership might change.

She trusts Donkey, who is one of her regular herb suppliers, and is more forthcoming with him than she might be with strangers.

---

## Views on the Oni

Liwen is quietly worried about the transition of power. She doesn't believe the change will be for the better, but keeps these thoughts to herself. She fulfilled [Magistrate Kurosawa's](magistrate-kurosawa.md) order without complaint and did not volunteer information about it—though she shared the details when Donkey asked directly.

---

## The Lily Order

[Magistrate Kurosawa](magistrate-kurosawa.md) personally visited Liwen and ordered **five dozen white lilies**—sixty flowers traditionally associated with funerals. When she asked the purpose, he said only that it was **"an oni custom"** and declined to elaborate. The specific quantity (five dozen) echoes the recurring pattern of fives connected to the ley lines and the ritual.

Liwen found the order unusual and mentioned it to [Radiant Willow](radiant-willow.md). She fulfilled it, delivered the lilies to the magistrate, and chose not to ask further questions.

---

## Relationships

### Donkey
Liwen and Donkey have a regular supplier relationship. He brings her herbs and new plant specimens from his foraging trips, and she trusts his judgment on botanical matters. She was forthcoming with him about the lily order when he visited.

### [Radiant Willow](radiant-willow.md)
Willow is one of Liwen's primary customers, purchasing flowers for Dew Drop Petals.
