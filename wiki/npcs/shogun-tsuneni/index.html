    Shogun Tsuneni¶

    The undisputed ruler of Chu Ye. No one has come close to challenging his power.

    Overview¶
    Name Tsuneni (TSOO-neh-nee)
    Title Shogun of Chu Ye
    Type Void Oni (Voidlord)
    Alignment Tyrannical
    Location Jyito (capital)

    Description¶

    Shogun Tsuneni is a void oni of immense power—the undisputed ruler of Chu Ye. He overthrew the old order when Imperial Lung Wa fell and has maintained his grip on the nation for a century. His rule is absolute; no challenger has ever come close to threatening his position.

    He convenes a ruling council in Jyito once a year to discuss affairs of state—a lavish event designed to appeal to the hedonistic nature of oni, making attendance something officials desire rather than dread.

    Government¶

    Tsuneni rules through two lieutenants who manage day-to-day governance:

    • Steward Imora (Storm Oni, Marshal) — Oversees the military
    • Steward Tanyo (Void Oni, Councilor) — Manages the bureaucracy

    The ruling council contains several factions that Tsuneni enjoys pitting against each other:

    Faction Leader Philosophy
    Daiyoku Uno (Caldera Oni) Militaristic; seeks to conquer all Tian Xia
    Shinkaze Shiga (Storm Oni) Isolationist; seeks spiritual redemption
    Hedonists Tamami (Village Oni) Pleasure-seekers; no interest in ruling

    Relevance to Willowshore¶

    Willowshore has been without an oni mayor for generations—a small, forgotten village allowed to govern itself. Now, a new oni mayor is arriving, bringing Tsuneni's regime directly into the lives of the villagers for the first time in living memory.

    The arrival has stirred unrest. The old ruling family refuses to participate in the traditional Revival of Friendship ceremony. Tensions are rising.
